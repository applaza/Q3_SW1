Friends

Friends is an American situation comedy about six friends who live in the same Manhattan apartment complex. It ran for ten seasons on NBC from September 22, 1994 to May 6, 2004.

Joey is the spinoff to Friends that focuses on the story Joey Tribbiani after the conclusion of Friends. It originally aired on NBC from September 9, 2004 - August 23, 2006, lasting two seasons before it was cancelled.



Characters


Rachel Greene



Is one of the show's main characters. Jennifer Aniston, who played her, won an Emmy and a Golden Globe for her performance.

Monica Geller



Courteney Cox played one of the main characters on the popular sitcom Friends. Monica was the group's "Mother Hen," and her Greenwich Village apartment served as one of the group's main gathering spots throughout the series.

Phoebe Buffay



Lisa Kudrow plays one of the main characters on the popular sitcom Friends. She is best known in Central Perk for her bohemian lifestyle, silly wit, and guitar playing.

Ross Geller



Was one of the show's main characters. David Schwimmer portrayed him. He's best known for his nerdy personality and awkward sense of humor, which reminds me of Screech from Saved By The Bell or David from Beverly Hills, 90210.

Chandler Bing



Is a central character on the popular sitcom Friends. Matthew Perry portrays him. He is known for his witty and sarcastic sense of humor.

Joey Tibbriani



Was a main character on Friends and the title character in the spin-off Joey. He is best known for being a ladies' man with a silly but endearing personality. Matt LeBlanc portrayed him.
